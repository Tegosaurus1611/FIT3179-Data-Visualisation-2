Paris Olympics 2024

In 2024 Paris hosted the 30th Summer Olympic games from July 26th to August 2nd. Athletes from around travelled to compete in a variety of sports across 48 disciplines.

Including the practices, heats, elimination rounds, semis and finals the 2024 Olympics had around 3000 watchable events. The heatmap below outlines the daily event schedule by discipline.

Although the Olympics have many events, not all events are medal-winning events. Certain sports such as Athletics and Swimming have many more medal winning events compared to sports like Golf and Volleyball.

The all-rounders
USA, China, Great Britain, France, Australia and Japan were the top 6 performing countries in terms of medal achievements. Athletes from these countries took home a well rounded bance of Gold, Silver and Bronze medals making their countries proud.

A total of 10,500 Athletes travelled from all around the world to compete at the Paris olympics.

It's interesting to note that countries with higher percentages of Athletes such as USA, China, Great Britain brought home more medals overall
